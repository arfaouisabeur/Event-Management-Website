Here is our event schedule

09:00 AM

Check-In & Welcome Zone

Inscription, soft music, photo corner, and Icebreaker cards.

10:00 AM

Cérémonie d’ouverture

Opening remarks, event objectives, agenda overview, and partner acknowledgments.

10:20 AM

Keynote Speech

Theme: “Le futur digital : E-commerce & opportunités pour la jeunesse tunisienne”.

11:00 AM

Panel 1 : E-commerce

Discussion on online selling, customer behavior, tools, and Q&A session.

11:45 AM

Activity 1 : Speed Networking

Dynamic rotations, introductions, selfies challenge, and a gift for the winner.

12:00 PM

Coffee Break

Coffee, juice, snacks, light music, and a photo corner.

12:30 PM

Panel 2 : Entrepreneuriat

Turning ideas into projects, success stories, discipline, and Q&A.

13:15 PM

Activity 2 : Business Idea Box

Participants submit business ideas; speakers select and comment on top 3 ideas.

13:45 PM

Coffee Break

Soft background music and networking time.

14:15 PM

Panel 3 : Freelancing

Participants scan a QR code and answer reflection questions.

Cérémonie de clôture

Event summary, acknowledgments, group photo, and invitation to future AIESEC events.
